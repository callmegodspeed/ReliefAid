About Us

ReliefAid is a non-profit organization dedicated to providing real-time disaster updates and relief coordination to communities in crisis. Our mission is to save lives, reduce suffering, and support recovery efforts during emergencies.

Our Mission

To deliver timely and accurate disaster information, coordinate relief efforts, and empower communities to rebuild and recover.

Our Vision

A world where no one is left behind during disasters, and every community has access to the resources they need to thrive.
